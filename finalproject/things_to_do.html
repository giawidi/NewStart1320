Things To Do

Chattanooga, Tennessee, offers a diverse array of attractions that cater to various interests. Here are some notable highlights:

Outdoor & Adventure Activities

  • Lookout Mountain – Home to Rock City, Ruby Falls, and the Incline Railway, offering stunning views and underground waterfalls.
  • Chattanooga Riverwalk – A scenic 13-mile paved trail along the Tennessee River, perfect for biking and walking.
  • Raccoon Mountain Caverns – A cave system for guided tours and spelunking adventures.
  • Enterprise South Nature Park – Miles of trails for hiking, mountain biking, and wildlife viewing.

Family-Friendly Attractions

  • Tennessee Aquarium – One of the best aquariums in the U.S., featuring both freshwater and saltwater exhibits.
  • Creative Discovery Museum – A hands-on museum for kids with interactive exhibits in science and art.
  • Lake Winnepesaukah Amusement Park – A classic amusement park with rides and a water park.
  • 11. Chattanooga Zoo - Over 500 animals, representing over 150 different species, many of which are endangered. Get face to face with a chimpanzee or jaguar, feed a giraffe, or check out some rare and venomous reptiles!

Arts, History & Culture

  • Hunter Museum of American Art – Features a stunning collection of American art with breathtaking river views.
  • Tennessee Valley Railroad Museum – Offers historic train rides through scenic landscapes.
  • Chattanooga Choo Choo – A historic train station turned entertainment complex with restaurants and attractions.
  • Bluff View Art District – A charming area with galleries, cafés, and beautiful river views.
